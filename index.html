Hocan Logistics

Optimized for delivery in urban centers across Kenya and East Africa. We started in Nairobi and have built our operations around the unique demands of city logistics.

From last-mile delivery in dense neighbourhoods to cross-town freight, Hocan Logistics is built for urban efficiency. Our Nairobi roots mean we know the routes, the traffic patterns, and the local requirements that keep your packages moving on time.

We offer same-day and next-day delivery in major urban centers, real-time tracking, and dedicated account management for businesses of every size. Whether you need regular replenishment runs or one-off urgent shipments, our fleet and hubs are positioned to serve you.

Hocan Supplies

We deliver at scale through major contracts, tenders, and long-term supply agreements. Our capability and capacity are built for large-volume and institutional clients.

Hocan Supplies is equipped to handle big contracts and competitive tenders. We combine robust procurement, reliable distribution, and compliance-ready processes so you can meet your obligations on time and to spec.

From office and facility supplies to industrial and project-based procurement, we bring the capability to support government, corporate, and NGO tenders. Our team manages end-to-end logistics so you can focus on your core work.
